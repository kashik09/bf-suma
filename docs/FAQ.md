## Usage & Safety

### How should I store BF Suma products?
Most products should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Check individual product labels for specific storage instructions. Keep all products out of reach of children.

### Can I take multiple supplements together?
While many of our products can be used together, we recommend introducing one new product at a time to observe how your body responds. For specific combinations, consult with a healthcare professional.

### What if I experience side effects?
Discontinue use immediately and consult a healthcare professional. While our products are generally well-tolerated, individual reactions can vary. Always follow recommended dosages.

### Are BF Suma products safe during pregnancy?
We recommend consulting your doctor before using any supplements during pregnancy or breastfeeding. Some products may not be suitable during this time.

### Do products have expiration dates?
Yes. All products have expiration dates printed on the packaging. Do not use products past their expiration date.
